			My Story?  :)
				- I was born and raised in Singapore in a Chinese family.
				- As part of the curriculum of the secondary school I went to, we needed to read a
				  book everyday for 20mins in the morning, and this was what started me on my journey.
				- My interest in electronics begin when I read a book (I forgot what was the book's title)
				  and it talked about the foxhole radio and the line, "the foxhole radio does not need any
				  power to operate", that really got my interest. I was really amazed at the concept of
				  wireless power/data transmission and started to learn more about radios by reading
				  up on them. If you know, as foxhole radio is a radio that does not need any power to
				  operate, because it operates entirely off the power of the radio waves in the air.
				  That amazed me because I never heard of something that is entirely wirelessly
				  powered before. I decided to try and build my very own foxhole radio and through
				  that process my interest in electronics grew and I started to delve deeper into
				  it and started learning about arduino as my first microcontroller.
				- Found my mentor online, at that time he was a undergrad from NUS doing a degree
				  in Computer Science and Math. Started learning C programming from him and started
				  delving into competitve programming with the UVA online judge.
				- Since then I have been constantly learning more about programming and electronics
				  and started doing interesting DIY projects on my own.
