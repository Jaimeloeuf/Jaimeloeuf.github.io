		Fan controller    Source Code
			About:
				An automatic DIY fan controller that controls the fan base on temperature
				of the surrounding air at night to prevent the fan from being on all
				night which will recude power usage, and prevent room from being too hot.
			Tech:
				- Embedded C (Basically low level programming)
				- AVR Chip used as the microcontroller with the Atmel toolchain.
				- Self designed electronics circuit that includes the touch sensor
				  as switches, the battery controller and the wireless communication
				  system to connect to the fan wirelessly.
